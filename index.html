About Us

Welcome to Sake Sushi, a cozy all-you-can-eat sushi spot at 2347 86th St, Brooklyn, NY 11214. Renowned for its fresh quality and generous portions, it's a local favorite for both lunch ($19.95) and dinner ($29).

Sushi lovers praise the perfect fish-to-rice ratio and expertly crafted rolls. “The best AYCE sushi spot in the tri-state,” says Chelsea L. The atmosphere is warm and family-friendly, with attentive service throughout. Options include vegan dishes and a few vegetarian choices. Enjoy wheelchair access, indoor/outdoor seating, and a relaxed vibe. Payments are easy—credit cards, Apple Pay, and Android Pay are accepted. Takeout and delivery make it easy to enjoy from home. Happy hour specials add extra value and fun. For a quieter experience, visit Mon–Thu during off-peak hours. Call (718) 996-6288 to reserve your table and savor sushi bliss.
